👋 Introduction

Meet the Creator

Hello! I'm Anuprit Patil, a Class 10 CBSE student with a deep passion for both academics and technology. I believe that the way students prepare for their Board examinations needs to be completely reimagined — away from passive reading and towards active, data-driven, goal-oriented study.

Board Mastery was born from my personal frustration with existing study tools. Most platforms are cluttered, distraction-heavy, or too generic to be genuinely useful for CBSE preparation. I wanted something premium — a platform that respects a student's time and intelligence.

This project represents everything I believe in: discipline, focus, creativity, and the relentless pursuit of excellence. I built every feature, wrote every line of code, and researched every note to give my fellow students the best possible study companion.

🗺️ Vision & Journey

Why I Built Board Mastery

💭
The Problem I Saw
Fellow students were struggling with scattered notes, no study structure, and anxiety about board exams. Most study apps were either too simple or too expensive. There was a gap: a free, professional-grade tool built specifically for CBSE Class 10.
🔬
The Research Phase
I spent weeks studying CBSE syllabi across all 5 subjects, learning about effective study techniques like spaced repetition, active recall, and the Pomodoro technique. I wanted every feature to be backed by science, not just gut feeling.
⚒️
Building the Platform
I taught myself web development — HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — and applied what I learned to build a fully functional platform. Every quiz question, every note, every formula was personally researched and written. This isn't just a website; it's my best work.
🚀
Launching Board Mastery
Board Mastery launched as a complete platform with a Study Planner, Notes Hub, Quiz Zone, and Achievement system. My goal is for every CBSE Class 10 student to walk into their board exams prepared, confident, and proud.
🔮
Future Goals
I plan to expand Board Mastery to Class 11 and 12, add AI-powered personalized study plans, connect with a community of students, and eventually build one of India's most impactful edtech platforms. This is just the beginning.
