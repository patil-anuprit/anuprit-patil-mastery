11. Importance of pH in Everyday Life

Human Body

Blood normally maintains pH around 7.4. Even small changes can be dangerous.

Digestive System

Stomach contains Hydrochloric Acid. Excess acid causes indigestion. Antacids help neutralize excess acid.

Tooth Decay

When mouth pH falls below 5.5, tooth enamel starts dissolving.

Soil pH

Plants grow best in suitable soil pH. Farmers use lime to reduce soil acidity.
